Chapter 3

Image, Temperament & Health (形象与性情)

3.1 Image

The chart image is Fire-Earth dominant with Metal expression — a mountain that contains precious ore. The structure is relatively clear (清): strong Earth produced by Fire, expressing through Metal. No major clashes disrupt the core structure.

3.2 Temperament

Earth dominant — solid build, deliberate movements, calm presence. Wu Earth people are the anchors in any room: reliable, nurturing, slow to anger but immovable once decided. You are a natural mediator who prefers stability over drama.

The Direct Wealth pattern adds a practical, detail-oriented streak. You don't dream of abstract riches — you calculate, plan, and build. The Xin Metal in the month stem gives you an analytical precision that others find impressive and occasionally intimidating.

3.3 Health Notes

Digestive system and spleen are the vulnerable areas — Earth governs these in Five Element medicine. The absence of Water in the chart suggests kidney and urinary system should be monitored. Avoid overthinking (思伤脾) and damp environments.

Chapter 4

Family Relations & Wealth/Career (六亲与财官)

4.1 Family (Six Relations)

Parents: Metal is strong in the chart — the father figure (represented by the controlling element) is influential. The Year pillar's Geng Metal suggests a structured, possibly strict upbringing. The Month pillar contains the mother's influence through the Resource element (Fire producing Earth).

Partner: For a female Wu Earth chart, the partner is represented by Wood (the controlling element). Wood is absent from the chart — this doesn't mean no marriage, but it suggests the partner may appear later or be less prominent in the early life. A partner with strong Water element is ideal — they balance Earth's solidity with flow and adaptability.

4.2 Wealth

Direct Wealth (正财) pattern — consistent, steady accumulation rather than sudden windfalls. Xin Metal penetrates from the month, confirming an income derived from professional skill and structured effort. Wealth peaks occur during Metal and Water luck cycles, when the chart's elements are balanced.

The Fire-rich chart means wealth is produced through creative output and visibility — careers in media, technology, or structured environments (finance, consulting) align well.

4.3 Career

Fire produces Earth → Earth produces Metal. This is a creative-to-practical pipeline. You're built for roles where you can take something abstract (Fire ideas) and make it real (Metal results). Management, operations, project leadership — any role that requires both vision and execution.

Supplementary Calculations (补充推算)

6.1 Life Palace (命宫)

Shen (申) Palace. The Life Palace sits in the Monkey position, containing Geng Metal (本气), Ren Water (中气), and Wu Earth (余气). This is a favorable palace — it brings Metal and Water, both favorable elements for this chart. The Shen palace suggests travel, intellectual pursuits, and cross-cultural connections.

6.2 Auspicious Stars (神煞)

Tian Yi Nobleman (天乙贵人): In Chou (丑) and Wei (未) — helpful people appear during Ox and Goat years. Network-building in these periods yields unexpected opportunities.

Traveling Horse (驿马): In Shen (申) — a strong indicator of mobility. Career or life changes involving travel, relocation, or international connections are structurally supported.

6.3 Illness & Remedy (病药说)

The illness (病): Wood controls Earth, but Wood is absent from the chart. The imbalance is not from Wood attack but from Fire overproducing Earth — the chart is too hot and dry.

The remedy (药): Water and Metal. Water cools the excess Fire; Metal drains the surplus Earth and provides expression. Years and luck cycles with Water or Metal are the natural medicine for this chart.

Leverage Metal luck — finance, technology, structured environments. Your chart excels in domains where precision, reliability, and long-term thinking are valued. Avoid purely creative or speculative fields — your wealth comes through building, not gambling. The Traveling Horse in your Life Palace supports international opportunities — don't limit yourself geographically.

Golden Window

2027–2032. The Ding Chou (丁丑) luck cycle brings Direct Resource — a period of consolidation, authority, and compounding returns. The Chou branch activates your Tian Yi Nobleman, bringing unexpected support. This is your decade to build something lasting. Act before the Wood luck incoming (after 2037) shifts the terrain.

Caution Points

2026 (Bing Wu) — avoid over-commitment. Fire-heavy years bring stagnation for Earth-dominant charts. Don't make major financial decisions this year. Focus on skill-building, networking, and maintaining existing positions. The pressure is real but temporary — the next year (2027) marks the beginning of your strongest period.

Relationships: Your chart's Earth dominance can create a tendency toward emotional rigidity. The absence of Wood (the controlling element) means you may struggle with vulnerability. A partner who brings Water — adaptability, emotional intelligence — is your ideal complement.

Life Theme

The Steadfast Builder. Your chart is not one of sudden brilliance but of patient, compounding excellence. You are the mountain that grows taller with each season — not the lightning that flashes and fades. Your greatest strength is persistence. Your greatest risk is stubbornness. Build slowly, build well, and the chart promises a life of substance.
